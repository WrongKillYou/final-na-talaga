ABOUT

Nurturing young minds for a brighter community future.

The Brgy. Anonas Daycare Center is a safe, nurturing, and child-friendly learning space dedicated to supporting the early development of young children in the community. We provide a caring environment where children are encouraged to learn, play, and grow through age-appropriate activities that build social, emotional, and basic academic skills. Guided by committed daycare workers and supported by the barangay, the center aims to help every child develop confidence, curiosity, and a love for learning as a strong foundation for their future education.

FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Brgy. Anonas Daycare Center.

What are the daycare center's operating hours?

We operate Monday to Friday from 7:30 AM to 4:30 PM, excluding public holidays.

What age group does the daycare accept?

We accept children aged 3 to 5 years old, focusing on early childhood development.

What programs and activities are offered?

Our programs include structured learning, play-based activities, arts, music, and social development exercises.

How can I enroll my child?

Enrollment can be done at the barangay hall or directly at the daycare center. Please bring your child's birth certificate and other necessary documents.
